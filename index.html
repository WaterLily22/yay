Chocolate Cake!!

hehehe idrk what im doing but chocolate cake was the first thing i baked so i made a recipe page for it lol

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup yogurt or 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 3 tbsps cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350° F
  2. Line an 8" cake tin with parchment paper
  3. Whisk the yogurt/egg and sugar in a bowl
  4. Whisk in the oil
  5. Sift in the baking powder, baking soda, and cocoa powder and whisk until combined
  6. Sift in the flour and fold with a spatula
  7. Fold in the chocolate chips
  8. Transfer the batter into the cake tin and bake for 25 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean
